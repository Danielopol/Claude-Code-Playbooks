## LinkedIn Message Templates

### Connection Request
```markdown
## Template: [Situation]

**Length:** <300 characters

Hi [Name],

[Personalization hook - why you're reaching out to THEM specifically]

[Value prop or reason to connect - not a pitch]

[Soft CTA]

[Your name]
```

### Examples by Hook Type

**Mutual Connection:**
```
Hi Sarah, noticed we both know [Name] from [Context]. I'm working on [relevant topic] and thought it'd be great to connect given your work at [Company]. Always enjoy comparing notes with others in the space.
```

**Recent Company News:**
```
Hi John, saw [Company] just [news hook]. Congrats! I've been following your growth in [area]. Would love to connect - I work with similar companies on [relevant topic].
```

**Content Engagement:**
```
Hi Maria, loved your post about [topic]. Your point about [specific insight] really resonated. I'm exploring similar ideas at [Company]. Would be great to connect.
```

**Shared Background:**
```
Hi Alex, fellow [school/company/group] alum here! Noticed you're now leading [function] at [Company]. Would love to connect - always great to see where people from [shared experience] end up.
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

```markdown
## Follow-Up Strategy

### Day 1: Connection accepted
Wait 24 hours before messaging

### Day 2: First message
Brief, value-first message
No pitch, no ask
Thank for connecting

### Day 5-7: Second touch
Share relevant content
Ask a question about their work
Build relationship

### Day 10-14: Soft pitch
If engagement positive
Mention how you help similar companies
Offer specific value (insight, intro, resource)

### Day 21+: Value nurture
If no response, switch to long-game
Engage with their content
Stay visible, stay helpful
```

## Best Practices

### Do
- ✅ Personalize every message
- ✅ Lead with value, not pitch
- ✅ Research before reaching out
- ✅ Engage with their content first
- ✅ Be patient - nurture relationships

### Don't
- ❌ Send generic templates
- ❌ Pitch in connection request
- ❌ Automate without personalization
- ❌ Follow up too aggressively
- ❌ Connect without a reason
