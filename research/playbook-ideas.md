# Playbook Ideas for Claude Code Playbooks

## Priority 1: High-Impact, Well-Documented

These have the most source material and clear CLAUDE.md templates available.

### 1. Invoice & Tax Document Organizer
**Category:** `finance-accounting`
**Difficulty:** `beginner`
**Time:** 5 minutes

**What it does:**
- Reads files in a messy folder
- Renames to `YYYY-MM-DD_Vendor_Invoice_Description.pdf`
- Sorts into appropriate folders

**Source:** Lenny's Newsletter, MasteringAI
**Template available:** Yes (simple)

---

### 2. Obsidian Vault Assistant
**Category:** `note-management`
**Difficulty:** `intermediate`
**Time:** 15 minutes

**What it does:**
- Automated backlinking for daily notes
- Find and connect related notes
- Summarize folder contents
- Maintain frontmatter consistency

**Source:** Claudesidian, Kyle Gao's guide, Axton Liu
**Template available:** Yes (multiple versions)

---

### 3. Downloads Folder Organizer
**Category:** `file-organization`
**Difficulty:** `beginner`
**Time:** 5 minutes

**What it does:**

- Creates meaningful folder structure
- Moves files to appropriate locations
- Identifies duplicates

**Source:** MasteringAI, Lenny's Newsletter
**Template available:** Yes (simple)

---

### 4. CSV/Excel Data Analyst
**Category:** `file-organization`
**Difficulty:** `beginner`
**Time:** 5 minutes

**What it does:**
- Answer business questions from spreadsheets
- Generate summaries for leadership
- No VLOOKUP/pivot tables needed
- Create charts and visualizations

**Source:** Claude Code for Non-Coders, Claude for Excel
**Template available:** Partial

### 5. Meeting Notes to Action Items
