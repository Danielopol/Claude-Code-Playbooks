## What This Does

Generate cold emails that feel genuinely personal - not mail-merge "Hi {FirstName}" spam. Claude researches each prospect's company news, LinkedIn, and content to find real hooks.

---

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Download the Template

Click **Download** above to get the `CLAUDE.md` file.

### Step 2: Prepare Your List

Have a list with: Name, Company, Title, LinkedIn URL

### Step 3: Share Your Voice

Give Claude 5-10 emails you've sent before.

### Step 4: Generate

Say: **"Write personalized emails to this list"**

---

## Personalization Levels

| Level | What's Included | Example |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| **Basic** | Name, company, industry | "Hi Jane at Acme..." |
| **Good** | + Recent news, role pain point | "Congrats on the Series B..." |
| **Excellent** | + Personal detail, specific hook | "Loved your podcast on scaling..." |

---

## Research Per Prospect

For each person, Claude finds:
- Recent company news/funding
- Their LinkedIn posts or articles
- Industry trends affecting them
- Timing hooks (why reach out NOW)

## Voice Matching

Claude analyzes your emails for:
- Formality (Hey vs Hi vs Dear)
- Sentence length and rhythm
- Sign-off style
- Humor level

---

## Tips

- **Quality > quantity**: 10 great emails beat 100 generic ones
- **Specific hooks**: "Your LinkedIn post about X" > "I see you're in sales"
- **Soft CTAs**: "Worth a chat?" > "Book a demo now"
- **You still send**: Claude drafts, you review and send

## Troubleshooting

**Emails sound generic**
Provide more examples of your writing style

**Can't find good hooks**
Try: "Focus on their recent LinkedIn activity"

**Taking too long**
Process in smaller batches: "Do 5 prospects first"
