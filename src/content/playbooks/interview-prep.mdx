## What This Does

Comprehensive interview preparation including company research briefs, a library of STAR-format stories for behavioral questions, practice with common questions, and post-interview debriefing.

## Prerequisites

- Claude Code installed
- Job posting for the role
- Your resume and work history
- Interview date (for scheduling prep)

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Download the Template

Download the CLAUDE.md template below and save it to your interview prep folder.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Prep Folder

```
interview-prep/
├── CLAUDE.md
├── companies/
│   └── google-pm-role/
├── star-stories.md
├── common-questions.md
└── debriefs/
```

### Step 3: Start Preparing

Begin your interview prep:

```
Prepare me for my Product Manager interview at Google next Tuesday
```

## Example Usage

```
"Research Stripe for my interview"

```

## What Gets Prepared

- **Company research**: Business model, recent news, culture
- **STAR stories**: 5-7 versatile achievement stories
- **Question practice**: Behavioral, situational, technical
- **Your questions**: Smart questions to ask them
- **Logistics**: Day-of checklist

## Best Practices

1. **Practice out loud** - Don't just read your answers
2. **Record yourself** - Review for filler words, pace
3. **Prepare 5-7 stories** - Versatile for many questions
4. **Research thoroughly** - Know their products, news, values
5. **Prepare questions** - Shows genuine interest
