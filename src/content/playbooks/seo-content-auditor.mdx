## What This Does

Comprehensive SEO content audit that would take days manually - analyze content quality, find technical issues, identify keyword cannibalization, and prioritize fixes by impact.

---

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Download the Template

Click **Download** above to get the `CLAUDE.md` file.

### Step 2: Export Your Data

Export from Google Search Console + Analytics (or provide site access).

### Step 3: Run Audit

```bash
claude
```

Say: **"Audit my site's SEO content"**

---

## What Gets Audited

| Area | What's Checked |
|------|----------------|
| **Technical** | Titles, metas, headers, URLs |
| **Content** | Thin pages, freshness, quality |
| **Keywords** | Cannibalization, opportunities |
| **Performance** | Traffic trends, CTR, rankings |

## Quick Wins Identified

| Fix | Effort | Impact |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Add missing meta descriptions | Low | Medium |
| Fix duplicate titles | Low | High |
| Add FAQ schema | Low | Medium |
| Update old high-traffic posts | Medium | High |

## Data Sources

**Required:**
- Google Search Console export
- Google Analytics export

**Optional:**
- Ahrefs/SEMrush data
- Screaming Frog crawl
- Content inventory spreadsheet

---

## Tips

- **Fix critical first**: Duplicate titles and thin content
- **Quick wins matter**: Meta descriptions improve CTR fast
- **Consolidate > create**: Merge competing pages
- **Fresh content ranks**: Update old posts yearly

## Troubleshooting

**No Search Console data**
Claude can still audit on-page elements from URLs

**Too many issues**
Focus: "Just show me the top 10 highest-impact fixes"

**Not sure what to fix first**
Claude prioritizes by effort vs impact automatically
